Print Artifacts Caused by PrusaSlicer's Arachne Wall Generator

Case be75e0be6f1f · 3dprinting

#PrusaSlicer#slicer settings#Arachne#artifacts#wall generator#G-code#troubleshooting

Проблема

A user experienced consistent print artifacts at the same Z-height on their models. Upon inspection in PrusaSlicer, they discovered the slicer was generating these artifacts in the G-code preview, even though they were absent from the original STL file.

Рішення

The issue was identified as being caused by the "Arachne" wall generation algorithm in PrusaSlicer. The community advised disabling Arachne, as it can be buggy and cause unexpected printing issues. Switching back to the "Classic" wall generator is the recommended solution unless Arachne is specifically required for a particular model.
