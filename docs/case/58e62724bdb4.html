Resetting Unresponsive Athom Tasmota Light Bulbs (LB01-15W-E27)

Case 58e62724bdb4 · tasmota

#athom#bulb#LB01-15W-E27#reset#unresponsive#recovery#wifi#power cycle

Проблема

A user reported that 3 out of 4 Athom LB01-15W-E27 bulbs pre-flashed with Tasmota became unresponsive. The standard reset procedure of turning them on and off quickly (e.g., 6 times) did not work, and the bulbs showed no signs of life (did not light up).

Рішення

Another user suggested trying a slower power cycling sequence. The original user confirmed this worked. The successful procedure was to turn the power off and on between 5 to 7 times, with a longer interval of about 2 to 5 seconds for both the 'on' and 'off' states. It was also noted that after a successful reset, the bulb might not light up but will start broadcasting its Wi-Fi access point. One must check for the new Wi-Fi network to confirm the reset. The user also shared the official Tasmota documentation for device recovery: https://tasmota.github.io/docs/Device-Recovery/#recovery-techniques
