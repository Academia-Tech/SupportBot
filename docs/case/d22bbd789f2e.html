How to update WiFi credentials on a Tasmota device after changing SSID

Case d22bbd789f2e · tasmota

#WiFi#SSID#AP Mode#Device Recovery#Tasmota#credentials

Проблема

A user changed their router's SSID and now their Sonoff Dual R4 device cannot connect. They are asking how to get it back into AP mode to reconfigure the WiFi without physical access or a hard reset.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

Two methods were suggested to update Wi-Fi credentials. The first is a hard reset by power cycling the device 6-7 times to enter recovery mode, which triggers AP mode. The user confirmed this method worked by using the 'on/off 7 times procedure' as described in the Tasmota Device Recovery documentation: https://tasmota.github.io/docs/Device-Recovery/#fast-power-cycle-device-recovery. Another suggestion was to use 5 clicks on GPIO0 to transfer to hotspot mode.
