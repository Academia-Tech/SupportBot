Finding a More Capable Alternative to Sonoff RF433 Bridge

Case 2687eed39a55 · tasmota

#rf bridge#sonoff rf#openmqttgateway#ttgo lora#rflink#sdr#433mhz#rf blinds

Проблема

A user was struggling to control RF blinds using a Tasmota-flashed Sonoff RF433 Bridge, finding its device support very limited. Despite trying different firmwares and configurations, including RFLink forks and raw data transmission, they were unsuccessful.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

It was recommended to abandon the Sonoff RF433 Bridge as it is a 'complete waste of time and money' for complex RF devices. The primary recommendation is to use OpenMQTTGateway firmware on a more capable TTGO LORA433 board. Other suggested alternatives include the RFLink project (https://www.rflink.nl/) on an ATMega board, or using a Raspberry Pi with an SDR dongle for more advanced signal decoding.
