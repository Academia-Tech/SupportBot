SSR or 4-Channel Relay Not Working with Tasmota

Case 8812fc20e05e · tasmota

#relay#SSR#hardware#GPIO#troubleshooting#solder jumper#trigger level

Проблема

A user's new SSR relay, configured as a standard 'relay' on a GPIO, was not functioning. An older mechanical relay worked correctly in the same setup, leading to the suspicion that the new SSR was faulty or incompatible.

Рішення

The issue was traced to the 4-channel relay board itself, not the SSR. The board featured solder jumpers for each channel that determine the trigger logic (active high or active low). By changing the solder joints to match the logic level required by the setup, the relay began to work correctly.
