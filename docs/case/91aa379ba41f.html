Проблема

A user was looking for a reliable 3D printer alternative to Bambu Lab models. The key requirements were an 'out-of-the-box' experience, minimal tinkering, an enclosure, multi-material capabilities, and speed. The user was also open to high-end kits if they are reliable once built.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

Several suggestions were provided: 1. For high-end DIY kits like VZBot or Voron, it was suggested to find a local owner on their respective Discord servers who might be willing to build one for a fee. 2. The VZBot Discord has a specific channel where users can request printed parts from the community. 3. A specific pre-built printer was recommended that fits the requirements: the Elegoo Centauri Carbon. A link to the product page was provided: https://eu.elegoo.com/products/centauri-carbon
