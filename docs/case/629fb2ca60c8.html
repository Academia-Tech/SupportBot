Designing a 3D Printed Tilting Stand for an Instax Camera

Case 629fb2ca60c8 · 3dprinting

#3d printing#functional print#camera accessory#instax#design improvement#o-ring#petg#abs

Проблема

An Instax camera lacks a tripod mount and is unstable on uneven surfaces, making it difficult to position for group photos. A user needed to create a stable, tilting stand for it.

Рішення

A custom tilting stand was designed and 3D printed to hold an Instax camera securely. The initial design used small, printed teeth on the rotating part to create friction for holding the tilt angle. After a discussion on materials, the design was further improved with the idea of using standard o-rings. This allows for a stepless adjustment and adds plenty of resistance. The 3D model can be adjusted to have a fitting profile for a common o-ring size (e.g., 20mm).
