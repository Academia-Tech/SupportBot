Проблема

A user was looking for a Tasmota-compatible device similar to the Shelly 3EM but with more channels (ideally 8 or 12) for power consumption monitoring only, without the need for switching relays.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

Several options were discussed. While the Shelly Pro 4PM (4 channels) and various 8-channel relay boards were mentioned, they primarily focus on switching. For a more modular and monitoring-focused solution, Kincony boards were recommended. Specifically, a setup like the KC868-A6 board running Tasmota, combined with an SDM630 energy meter via RS485, was suggested as a powerful combination for multi-channel power monitoring. Links to Kincony and an 8-channel ESP8266 board on AliExpress were also provided.
