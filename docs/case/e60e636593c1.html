Проблема

A user is looking for interesting upgrade ideas for a stock Ender 3 v2 they are receiving. They want to fix it up for someone else and are looking for suggestions beyond the standard upgrades they have already performed on their primary machine.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

A CoreXY conversion was suggested as a significant upgrade, with a specific project on Printables mentioned: https://www.printables.com/model/254732-ender-3-v2-corexy-v13. The discussion also covered Z-axis solutions for such a build. Using a single stepper motor to drive two Z-axis lead screws via a closed-loop belt was recommended for synchronization. It was noted by another user that this type of conversion project is considered more difficult than building a VzBot kit from scratch.
