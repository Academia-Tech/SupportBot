Recommended UV-proof filament for outdoor use

Case 38fa8164fe12 · 3dprinting

#filament#ASA#outdoor#UV resistance#heated enclosure#material selection

Проблема

A user with a heated enclosure printer is looking for a filament suitable for outdoor applications that is resistant to UV degradation.

Рішення

ASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate) filament is recommended for UV-proof outdoor use. A discussion on brands followed: Polymaker ASA is often recommended in BOMs (e.g., for Sherpa mini, ZeroG), but some users find it is blended for easier printing at the cost of thermal resistance and strength. Alternatives suggested include Fillamentum, Flashforge, and Qidi for ASA. For ABS, Spectrum and Fillamentum were recommended, while eSun ABS+ was considered low quality. Fusion Filament ASA was also mentioned as a high-performance option requiring an 80°C chamber.
