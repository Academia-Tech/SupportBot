Case 4b4b8da54f21 · tasmota

#flashing#esp-12f#esp8266#tasmotizer#error#power supply#baud rate#cp2102

Проблема

A user was unable to flash an ESP-12F board using Tasmotizer or the online flashing tool, consistently receiving a "failed to initialize" error despite checking wiring and trying different cables and boards.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

Two main solutions were proposed to resolve the flashing error: 1. **Insufficient Power:** The 3.3V regulator on many USB-to-serial adapters may not provide enough current for the ESP. Using a separate, stable 3.3V power supply or a more robust programmer (e.g., Laskakit CH9102) was recommended. 2. **Baud Rate:** Poor cables or grounding can cause communication issues. It was suggested to try flashing at a slower baud rate, such as 115200, to improve stability.
