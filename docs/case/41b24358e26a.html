ESP32 Reboots When Specific GPIOs Configured as Buttons/Switches

Case 41b24358e26a · tasmota

#ESP32#Tasmota#GPIO#reboot#button#switch#troubleshooting#development support

Проблема

An ESP32 running Tasmota reboots automatically when GPIOs 34, 35, 36, or 39 are configured as buttons or switches, even though they are input-only. This issue does not occur when configured as relays.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

If an ESP32 reboots when specific GPIOs (like 34, 35, 36, 39) are configured as buttons or switches, but not as relays, it is recommended to contact the Tasmota development team on Discord or GitHub. Providing a circuit diagram with the issue report would be helpful for diagnosis.
