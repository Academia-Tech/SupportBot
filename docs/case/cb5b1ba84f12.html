How to Get a Smooth First Layer Without Bed Texture

Case cb5b1ba84f12 · 3dprinting

#PEI sheet#smooth first layer#bed surface#Ender 3#PETG#AliExpress#Energetic

Проблема

A user wants to print on a flexible PEI sheet but avoid picking up the bed's texture on the first layer. They are looking for a product recommendation for a smooth sheet for an Ender 3 that is compatible with PETG.

Рекомендація (не підтверджено)

To achieve a smooth first layer without texture, use a smooth PEI sheet. A reliable supplier is the 'Energetic' store on AliExpress (https://www.aliexpress.com/store/4542004), but be wary of fakes. For adhesion, a glue stick is generally not needed. However, it is highly recommended when printing with TPU, not to increase adhesion, but to act as a release agent. TPU can stick so strongly to smooth PEI that it can damage the sheet upon removal. Compared to other surfaces, smooth PEI is considered superior to textured PEI and glass, offering better adhesion and thermal transmission.
